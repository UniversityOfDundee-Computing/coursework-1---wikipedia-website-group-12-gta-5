Intro

Trevor was born in 1967. His father left when Trevor was 10. He spent most of his childhood in Canada. He was once a member of Canadian air force but was grounded due to his mental state. Shortly after, Trevor turned to a life of crime eventually developing an interest for robbery after meeting Michael. After years of successful heists, a heist gone wrong separates them.

Personality

Trevor is a vengeful, brutal, ruthless, sadistic, impetuous, and psychotic man, and described as very hard to work with and is prone to sudden and violent outbursts. He carries out his actions in an extremely relentless manner, however at his core he is a very honest person that never exhibits ant hypocrisy and follows his own principles.

Skills

Due to his previous career of a pilot in the military, Trevor is very adept in the flying of aircrafts and helicopters and is able to effectively "pilot under pressure" when the team need to make an escape after a heist. Trevor is a man of ambition and insanity and has been capable of fairly complicated, risky, and illegal stunts.

Intro

Michael was born in 1965 somewhere in the Midwest. His reasons and events leading up to his career path are also unknown at present. Afer Micheal and Trevor met, and fell apart, Michael and his wife Amanda and two children: Jimmy and Tracey, fled to Los Santos.

Personality

Michael is extremely cunning, thinking motions through carefully and precisely being sure to keep his true motivations a secret. He has various other traits, such as his composure, inflated ego, ease in making moral sacrifices, and his hypocrisy. Throughout the story he tries to live two lifes and this is what makes his downfall

Skills

The only known ability, which he shares with the two other protagonists of the game (Franklin and Trevor), is the ability to slow down time while shooting. This ability is called "Bullet Time" and is purely from a gameplay perspective.

Intro

Franklin (Born 1988) in South Los Santos with no money or education. At some point during his life he took up a life of dealing drugs and a gang banger but in 2008 at age 20, he was arrested for cocaine posession and carrying a concealed firearm. 5 years later Franklin was arrested again and convicted at trial for murder.

Personality

Franklin is described as being an ambitious young man and eager to take on new, and often illegal, new challenges. He also exhibits a lot of hope in the actions that he does, acting as a contrasting character to other protagonist Michael De Santa. He develops a strong relationship with Michael, often acting as a father-son duo.

Skills

As a young adult Franklin has gathered a wide variety of skills through the various jobs he has done and has a level of physical strength that the other protagonist lack. As a repo man at the Armenian Car Dealership he has become adept in stealing cars from unsuspecting owners and knows his way around the wheel of a car. He also has excellent aim with fire arms.
