Intro

Trevor was born in 1967. His father left when Trevor was 10. He spent most of his childhood in Canada. He was once a member of Canadian air force but was grounded due to his mental state. Shortly after, Trevor turned to a life of crime eventually developing an interest for robbery after meeting Michael. After years of successful heists, a heist gone wrong separates them.

Personality

Trevor is a vengeful, brutal, ruthless, sadistic, impetuous, and psychotic man, and described as very hard to work with and is prone to sudden and violent outbursts. He carries out his actions in an extremely relentless manner, however at his core he is a very honest person that never exhibits ant hypocrisy and follows his own principles.

Skills

Due to his previous career of a pilot in the military, Trevor is very adept in the flying of aircrafts and helicopters and is able to effectively "pilot under pressure" when the team need to make an escape after a heist. Trevor is a man of ambition and insanity and has been capable of fairly complicated, risky, and illegal stunts which include; jet skiing, murder, property destruction, and jumping from heights.

Intro

Michael was born in 1965 somewhere in the Midwest. His reasons and events leading up to his career path are also unknown at present. During his tenure as a very successful robber, he met ex-Canadian Air Force pilot Trevor Phillips. Upon discovering they both share an interest in the art of thievery, they pull of many successful heists. After the two split following a disagreement, Michael and his wife Amanda and two children: Jimmy and Tracey, fled to Los Santos.

Personality

Michael is renowned as a man of extreme cunning, thinking motions through carefully and precisely being sure to keep his true motivations a secret. Michael is very slow to anger. Equally well known are various other traits, such as his composure, inflated ego, ease in making moral sacrifices, and his hypocrisy. Throughout the story he tries to live two lifes and this is what proceeds his downfall

Skills

The only known ability, which he shares with the two other protagonists of the game (Franklin and Trevor), is the ability to slow down time while shooting. This ability is called "Bullet Time" and is purely from a gameplay perspective.
